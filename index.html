Session rules

The store session is temporary. It loads enough wardrobe intelligence to help with the decision, but it does not expose persistent account history, biometrics, or open-ended analytics to the retailer. Task-based testing in realistic contexts is recommended because it surfaces whether people actually understand this distinction. [web:64][web:72]
